Cook: 5min
Proofing Time: 1h
Ready In: 5h 5min

Recipe Video

INGREDIENTS

8 Servings

Filling

  • 2 cups of marshmallow
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp rice flour
  • 1 cup ice water
  • 1 tsp high heat oil
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil

Base

  • 2/3 cup of supreme flour
  • 1 tbsp milk water

Sauce

  • 2 packets soy-chili vinegar
  • 1 cup green onion
  • 1 cup ginger
  • 1 cup cooking wine

PREPARATIONS

Step 1/5

Mix the flour and water until combined, then knead until you get a smooth dough. Shape the dough into a ball and let it rest for 30 minutes. Shape the dough into a long roll, then divide it into 8 equal pieces.

Step 2/5

Once ready to roll, first cut all the ingredients you want to pack into a filling. Add all the ingredients for the pork belly into a large bowl and mix well. Add all the seasonings for the pork belly into a large bowl and mix until everything is combined.

Step 3/5

Once ready with the pork belly, add into cup of the filling mixture to make filling. Mix everything well until the mixture is smooth and well combined.

Step 4/5

To quickly mix the dumpling, first, place the wrapper on your palm and add 1/2 cup of the filling in the center of the wrapper. Next fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling and pinch to seal with your right hand seal with your fingers.

Step 5/5

Place the dumplings on a parchment paper-lined basket, place a over at half-way water, cover with a lid and steam for 8-10 minutes.
